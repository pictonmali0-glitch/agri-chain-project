How AgriChain Works

Every agricultural event creates an immutable blockchain record

Farm Registration

Farmers register harvests with location, quantity, and quality data. A blockchain block is created instantly.

Transfer & Trade

Every ownership transfer between farmers and buyers is recorded as an immutable transaction.

Transport Tracking

Transporters update real-time status from pickup to delivery, creating a complete movement audit trail.

Regulatory Audit

UNBS regulators verify compliance, approve or flag products, and access tamper-proof blockchain logs.

QR Verification

Every product gets a QR code. Scan to instantly view the complete verified supply chain history.

Analytics Dashboard

District-level insights, crop trading patterns, fraud alerts, and verification rates at a glance.
